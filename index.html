Phumelela is a non-profit organization that aims to provide educational resources, life skills, and recreational support to underprivileged children.

What we do

Phumelela Foundation is active around the world, using grant giving research, advocacy, impact investment, and strategic litigation to build a future where everyone can live with rights and dignity.

Educational Resources

We provide books, learning materials, and digital resources to support children's education.

Life Skills

We offer workshops and training programs to help children develop essential life skills.

Recreational Support

We organize sports, arts, and cultural activities to promote holistic development.

— Our mission

To enable every child to reach their full human potential.
